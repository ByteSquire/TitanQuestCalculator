Onslaught

When used as your default weapon attack, Onslaught charges up as you hit enemies causing increased damage. The first hit always charges Onslaught to the first level of the skill and it continues to charge until it reaches your current skill level. Onslaught dissipates rapidly when not attacking. {^y}Assign this skill to your left mouse button.
