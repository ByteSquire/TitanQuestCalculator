Shadow Link

Keeps the Occultist and nearby allies connected to the shadow realm, increasing their energy regeneration and imbuing their weapons with the ability to penetrate deeper and to damage the life of their targets. This connection comes at a cost; the continuous exposure to these energies weakens the user's resistance and slowly drains their life.
