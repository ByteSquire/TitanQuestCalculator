Shadow Stalker

Summons a powerful demon to assist the Occultist. Even outside of the shadow realm, the stalker is able to subdue enemies with a barrage of life-damaging attacks.^y^nAt level 5, the Stalker learns Shadow Strike - displaces himself through the nether, dealing massive damage and paralyzing the enemy.^y^nAt level 10, the Stalker learns Shadow Rush, a temporary aura that greatly increases the damage and durability of the Stalker and any nearby allies.^y^nAt level 15, the Stalker learns Shadow Plague : infects a target, reducing a portion of its life instantly and slowing it for a few seconds--effect can leap to nearby enemies.
